#### 2. The Identity & Governance Gap in Autonomous Agents
The latest industry report from Gravitee underscores a dangerous trend: adoption is outpacing control. With 81% of organizations past the planning phase but only 14% having full security approval, the "Identity Crisis" is real. - **Key finding**: 88% of organizations suspect or confirmed an AI security incident in the past year.
